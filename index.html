Your New Capabilities: Full Visibility

This system provides a powerful new set of senses for Orchid Scientific. You will be able to see, track, and understand activity in three critical areas.

Capability 1

Complete File Monitoring

This is the core of the system. We are setting up a "digital watchman" for every critical file in your company, whether it's on a shared server or a local PC.

What It Is:

Real-time tracking of any file creation, modification, or deletion. We'll know the "Who, What, and When" of every file change.

Why It's Important for Orchid Scientific:

This protects your intellectual property. It ensures that sensitive Word documents, Excel spreadsheets, and crucial drawing files are only being accessed by the right people at the right times. It also provides a full audit trail.

What You Will See:

A simple, searchable log on your dashboard showing entries like:
"User 'Parag' modified file 'C:\NAS-Drive\Projects\Project_X.docx' on PC 'Beta-05' at 2:19 PM"

Capability 2

Full PC Activity Monitoring

Beyond just files, the "silent helpers" on all 30 "Beta" PCs create a complete diary of all important system events.

What It Is:

A detailed log of all significant user and system actions, including successful and failed logins, new software installations, and security setting changes.

Why It's Important for Orchid Scientific:

This provides accountability and security. Orchid Scientific will be able to instantly see if an unauthorized person is trying to guess a password, or if an employee installs unapproved or potentially harmful software on a company PC.

What You Will See:

Immediate alerts on your dashboard for critical events like:
"Multiple login failures for user 'Admin' on PC 'Beta-12'" or "Software 'new_game.exe' was installed on 'Beta-21'"

Capability 3

Internet & Website Visibility

The system also monitors all network traffic, giving Orchid Scientific a clear picture of what internet resources are being accessed from company computers.

What It Is:

A log of all outgoing network connections from each "Beta" PC. This allows you to see what websites and online services are being used.

Why It's Important for Orchid Scientific:

This helps ensure company computers are being used for company purposes. It's also a critical security feature, as it can help identify if a PC is "calling home" to a known malicious website associated with a virus or hacker.

What You Will See:

Reports and searchable logs on your dashboard showing which PCs are connecting to which websites, helping you spot unusual or non-compliant activity.
